# City Permit & Placement Notes

Last updated: 2026-03-28

## General Rules (Bay Area Wide)

- **Private property**: Driveway placement typically requires no permit
- **Public street**: Most Bay Area cities require an encroachment permit
- **HOA communities**: May have additional placement/timing guidelines
- **Plywood recommended**: Under dumpster on all driveways to prevent surface damage
